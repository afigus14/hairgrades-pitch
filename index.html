Hairgrades

Find a stylist that will make you feel good about your hair.

Founder: Dr. Anne Figus

The Problem

  • Clients struggle to find stylists who understand their hair type, style preferences, and budget.
  • Reviews on general platforms are unreliable or outdated.
  • Stylists lack a dedicated space to showcase their portfolios and build reputation.

Our Solution

  • A dedicated stylist review and discovery platform.
  • Search by hair type, style, location, and ratings.
  • Verified reviews, stylist portfolios, and direct booking links.

Market Opportunity

  • U.S. salon and haircare market is valued at $47B+.
  • Over 1 million licensed cosmetologists.
  • Growing interest in specialized care for textured, natural, and treated hair.

Business Model

  • Free Listings: Stylists claim their profiles.
  • Premium Subscriptions: Featured listings, analytics, portfolio boost.
  • Sponsored Placements: Ads for salons and products.
  • Booking Commissions: % from integrated bookings.

Go-To-Market Strategy

  • Launch in 1–2 key cities (Chicago, Atlanta) with pre-seeded stylist profiles.
  • Partner with cosmetology schools, hair expos, and influencer stylists.
  • Offer 6 months of free premium access to early adopters.
  • Social campaign: #RatedByHairgrades
